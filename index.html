Our Mission

@bay is aimed towards increasing civic engagement among young communities, such as the educating them on the latest trending news surrounding the United States, walking users through how to register to vote, keeping them up to speed on the 2020 election, finding out where their representatives stand on certain issues, and how to get involved in activism.
Research has shown that those who are more educated and wealthy are more likely to vote. With the 2020 election coming up, voter turnout is more important than ever, and young people and millennials currently have the smallest turnout of any age group!
@bay aims to level the playing field so that everyone can be educated about important issues in their communities and feel empowered to head to the voting stations and express their opinions, about how to better their communities.


@bay has won the 2018 Congressional App Challenge, which is the most prestigious prize in student computer science. Participation in the challenge has grown exponentially and has reached underserved, diverse, and rural student populations.
